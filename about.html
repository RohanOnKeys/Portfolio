about

i am a computer science student and independent builder interested in browser agents, machine learning, systems, and software that stays useful when networks, interfaces, or assumptions break.

i like making complicated systems easier to inspect. better context for agents. more reliable movement of data. tools that expose the hidden parts before they turn into a very expensive mystery.

browser agents are interesting because interfaces were made for people first.

context systems are interesting because more text is not automatically more understanding.

network protocols are interesting because reality does not promise a stable connection.

based in india. building in public. mostly trying to make browsers cooperate.
